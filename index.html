The Bahamas

Overview

The Bahamas is an archipelago located in the Atlantic Ocean, consisting of over 700 islands, cays, and islets. Situated to the southeast of Florida and north of Cuba, it is renowned for its stunning beaches, clear waters, and vibrant coral reefs.

Geography

Location

Chain of islands in the North Atlantic Ocean, southeast of Florida, northeast of Cuba; note - although The Bahamas does not border the Caribbean Sea, geopolitically it is often designated as a Caribbean nation.

Climate

The climate in Bahamas is mostly Tropical marine moderated by warm waters of Gulf Stream.
